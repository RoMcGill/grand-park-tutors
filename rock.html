Grand Park Tutors offers small group tutoring sessions for adults, We Specialize in english speaking and pronunciation.

Lessons

Our English lessons for adult Vietnamese students take place once a week in a quiet cafe with a maximum class size of four students. Our passionate, hard-working, and friendly native English-speaking tutors with extensive teaching experience provide one-hour lessons for 200k VND per student. Our lessons focus on improving the four basic language skills of listening, speaking, writing, and reading. We foster a reassuring learning environment that enables students to learn quickly. Our tutors take cognizance of where the student excels and struggles and design and implement lessons accordingly. We assign reading homework to students, which will be reviewed in the next lesson session. For ESL students, speaking could be a difficult task, which our tutors can help with.

Lesson plan for intermediate learners

Famous Landmarks Around the World

Level: Intermediate

Objective: To improve speaking and pronunciation skills while learning about famous landmarks around the world.

Materials: Pictures of famous landmarks, a world map, and a pronunciation guide.

Warm-up (5 minutes):

• Introduce the topic of famous landmarks around the world.

• Ask students if they have ever visited any famous landmarks and which ones they would like to visit.

• Show pictures of famous landmarks and ask students to identify them.

Presentation (20 minutes):

• Show pictures of famous landmarks and ask students to describe them.

• Introduce new vocabulary related to the landmarks and practice pronunciation.

• Use a world map to show the location of each landmark and ask students to identify the country and city.

Practice (20 minutes):

• Divide students into pairs and ask them to choose a landmark to research.

• In pairs, students will prepare a short presentation about their chosen landmark, including its history, significance, and interesting facts.

• Encourage students to use the new vocabulary and practice pronunciation.

Production (15 minutes):

• Each pair will present their chosen landmark to the class.

• The class will ask questions and provide feedback on pronunciation.

Wrap-up (5 minutes):

• Review the new vocabulary and pronunciation.

• Ask students to share what they learned about famous landmarks around the world.

• Encourage students to visit famous landmarks in the future.
