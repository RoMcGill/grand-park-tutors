hiphop

is a style of music which came into existence in the United States during the mid- 1970s, and became a large part of modern pop culture during the 1980s. It consists of two main components: rapping and DJing

rock

Rock music is a broad genre of music that originated as "rock and roll" in the United States in the late 1940s and early 1950s, developing into a range of different styles in the mid-1960s and later, particularly in the United States and the United Kingdom.

pop

pop music is often distinguished from other subgenres by stylistic traits such as a danceable rhythm or beat, simple melodies and a repeating structure

country

Country is a genre of music that originated with blues, Its popularized roots originate in the Southern and Southwestern United States of the early 1920s.
