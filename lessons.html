Grand Park Tutors offers small group tutoring sessions for adults, We Specialize in english speaking and pronunciation.

Lessons

Our English lessons for adult Vietnamese students take place each night in a quiet cafe with a maximum class size of four students. Our passionate, hard-working, and friendly native English-speaking tutors with extensive teaching experience provide one-hour lessons for 300k VND per student. Our lessons focus on improving the four basic language skills of listening, speaking, writing, and reading. We foster a reassuring learning environment that enables students to learn quickly. Our tutors take cognizance of where the student excels and struggles and design and implement lessons accordingly. We assign reading homework to students, which will be reviewed in the next lesson session. For ESL students, speaking could be a difficult task, which our tutors can help with.
